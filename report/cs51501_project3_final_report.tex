\addto{\captionsenglish}{\renewcommand{\abstractname}{Problem}}
\Problem{Given the large sparse symmetric eigenvalue problem $Ax = \lambda Bx$, where $A$ and $B$ are symmetric matrices of order $n = 10^6$, and $B$ is symmetric positive definite. Use the Trace Minimization algorithms: TraceMIN and TraceMIN-Davidson for obtaining the smallest $p \ll n$ eigenpairs, and use TraceMIN for computing \textbf{all} the eigenpairs belonging to an interior interval $[a, b]$.}
